# JAI Lab Brand Guide v1.0

## Name

Use: **JAI Lab**  
Meaning: **Joy AI Lab**  
Positioning: **An open research initiative for molecular discovery**

## Primary tagline

**Building Open Infrastructure for Molecular Discovery**

## Extended description

JAI Lab develops open datasets, benchmarks, scientific software, and AI systems for molecular sensing, fluorescent probe discovery, analytical chemistry, and drug discovery.

## Voice

Clear. Scientific. Minimal. Confident. Avoid hype.

Use:
- open infrastructure
- molecular discovery
- scientific software
- benchmark-first research
- molecular sensing
- data-centric AI
- experimental validation

Avoid:
- “best in the world”
- exaggerated claims
- “Open AI”
- “Principal Investigator” unless formally applicable
