# JAI Lab Brand System v1.0

## Core identity

**JAI Lab**  
Meaning: **Joy AI Lab**  
Positioning: **An open research initiative for molecular discovery**

## Primary tagline

**Building Open Infrastructure for Molecular Discovery**

## Description

JAI Lab develops open datasets, benchmarks, scientific software, and AI systems for molecular sensing, fluorescent probe discovery, analytical chemistry, medicinal chemistry, and drug discovery.

## Voice

Clear, scientific, minimal, confident.

Use:
- open infrastructure
- molecular discovery
- scientific software
- benchmark-first research
- molecular sensing
- data-centric AI
- experimental validation

Avoid:
- “Open AI”
- exaggerated “best in world” claims
- “Principal Investigator” unless formally applicable
