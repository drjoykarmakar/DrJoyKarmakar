# Code of Conduct

JAI Lab is committed to creating a respectful, inclusive, and constructive research community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and skill levels.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Acknowledge uncertainty and limitations.
- Credit prior work and contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or hostile communication.
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of work.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Scientific claims that intentionally hide limitations or uncertainty.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainer through the repository issue system or the public contact channels listed in the organization README.
