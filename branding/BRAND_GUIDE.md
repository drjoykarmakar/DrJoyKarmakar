# JAI Lab Brand Guide

## Brand position

**JAI Lab** is an open research initiative building infrastructure for AI-powered molecular discovery.

## Core message

> Building the open foundation for AI-powered molecular discovery.

## Alternative taglines

- Open infrastructure for molecular discovery.
- The open research ecosystem for molecular AI.
- Making molecular discovery reproducible, programmable, and collaborative.

## Naming recommendation

Keep **JAI Lab**.

It is short, memorable, flexible, and brandable. Avoid renaming to generic alternatives such as ChemAI Lab, MolecularAI Lab, or OpenChem AI. If a more institutional name is needed later, consider **JAI Research**, **JAI Institute**, or **JAI Science**, but keep JAI as the core brand.

## Voice

- clear
- precise
- scientific
- ambitious
- open
- credible

Avoid hype without evidence. Use strong claims only when supported by outputs, publications, datasets, or software.

## Typography

Recommended web fonts:

- Inter
- IBM Plex Sans
- Source Sans 3

- JetBrains Mono
- IBM Plex Mono
- Fira Code

## Design principles

- Use dark navy backgrounds with bright scientific accents.
- Prefer diagrams over dense text.
- Use consistent icon style.
- Keep every README scannable.
- Lead with utility, not biography.
- Put the founder section near the bottom, after mission and programs.
